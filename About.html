Rivera Roasts was born from a love of community, craftsmanship, and, of course, coffee. What started as a small dream in a home kitchen has grown into a welcoming neighborhood café where quality and connection come first. We believe every cup tells a story — from the farmers who grow our beans with care, to the baristas who pour their heart into every shot. At Rivera Roasts, we source only ethically grown, small-batch beans, roasted to perfection to bring out bold, complex flavors. Whether you're a morning regular grabbing your daily espresso or a traveler discovering us for the first time, we aim to make your experience warm, memorable, and delicious. Our space is designed for gathering — a cozy corner for quiet moments or a lively hub to meet friends. From our hand-crafted drinks to our homemade pastries, everything we do is rooted in passion and purpose. Welcome to Rivera Roasts — where the coffee is strong, the people are kind, and you're always part of the family.

Meet the Team

Maria Rivera – Founder & Head Roaster

Maria started Rivera Roasts with nothing but a dream, a vintage espresso machine, and a deep respect for the craft of coffee. She sources every bean with care and roasts in-house to bring out unique, rich flavors in every blend.

Jamal Harris – Lead Barista

With a background in latte art competitions and a playlist for every shift, Jamal brings energy and expertise to every pour. He’s the face you’ll see at the counter most mornings — just ask for his signature cinnamon mocha.

Lena Tran – Baker & Vibes Manager

Lena’s pastries are the reason many customers return daily. From flaky croissants to vegan chocolate muffins, she keeps our menu fresh and seasonal. She's also the one behind the cozy decor and Sunday playlist.

Tyler Nguyen – Marketing & Social Media

If you've seen our Insta reels or caught wind of our monthly coffee tastings, that's Tyler’s work. He keeps the world connected to our shop and is always coming up with clever new promos (ask him about the “Roast & Relax” punch card).
